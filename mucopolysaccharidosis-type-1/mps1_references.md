**Note on model scope:** the mrgsolve model represents laronidase disposition as fast receptor(M6PR)-mediated plasma clearance (references #8-9) feeding a slowly-decaying tissue-retained active-enzyme pool that sustains inter-dose biochemical effect — this two-timescale structure is a modeling simplification designed to reproduce the qualitative and approximate quantitative treatment-effect ordering reported across references #9-11 and #14-21, rather than a validated population-PK/PD fit to individual patient-level trial data. Parameters governing investigational modalities (AAV9 CNS-directed gene therapy, lentiviral HSC gene therapy beyond reference #22's reported cohort, and genistein substrate-reduction therapy in MPS I specifically) are explicitly illustrative and flagged as such in the Shiny app and R model calibration notes.
