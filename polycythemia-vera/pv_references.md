## Summary of Key Clinical Trial Data for Model Calibration

| Trial | Drug | Endpoint | Result (drug vs control) | Reference |
|-------|------|----------|--------------------------|-----------|
| RESPONSE | Ruxolitinib | SVR35 at Wk32 | **38% vs 1%** | NEJM 2015 |
| RESPONSE | Ruxolitinib | HCT control Wk32 | **60% vs 20%** | NEJM 2015 |
| RESPONSE-2 | Ruxolitinib | HCT control Wk28 | **62% vs 19%** | Lancet Oncol 2017 |
| MAJIC-PV | Ruxolitinib | CHR at 1yr | **41% vs 26%** | Lancet Haematol 2017 |
| CYTOREDUCE | HCT <45% | Thrombosis/death | **HR 0.25 (4-fold ↓)** | NEJM 2013 |
| PROUD-PV | Ropeg-IFN | Non-inferior allele burden | **Allele ↓ vs HU** | Lancet Haematol 2020 |
| CONTINUATION-PV | Ropeg-IFN | Mol. response at 5yr | **~41% vs ~14% mol. remission** | Leukemia 2020 |
| ECLAP | Aspirin | Microvascular events | **HR 0.41 (60% ↓)** | NEJM 2004 |

*SVR35 = Spleen Volume Reduction ≥35%; CHR = Complete Hematologic Response; HCT = Hematocrit*
