# Autoimmune Hemolytic Anemia (AIHA) — QSP Model References

## Overview

AIHA is classified into warm AIHA (wAIHA, IgG-mediated, ~70%), cold agglutinin disease (CAD, IgM-mediated, ~15–20%), mixed-type (~5%), and rare subtypes (paroxysmal cold hemoglobinuria, drug-induced). Key pathomechanisms include autoantibody production, Fcγ receptor-mediated extravascular hemolysis, and complement-mediated intravascular hemolysis.
