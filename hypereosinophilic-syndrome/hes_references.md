# Hypereosinophilic Syndrome (HES) — Key References for the QSP Model

> Curated bibliography supporting the mechanistic map, mrgsolve ODE model,
> and Shiny dashboard. Citations are grouped by topic; an asterisk (★)
> marks **primary parameter/calibration anchors** for the model. All
> PMIDs were checked for plausibility against known author/journal/year
> combinations; users should re-verify against PubMed/NCBI before
> regulatory or clinical use.
