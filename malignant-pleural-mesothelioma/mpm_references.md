### Notes on model use

* Chemotherapy/anti-VEGF/immunotherapy PK parameters (clearance, volumes,
  half-lives) are literature-typical population values reconstructed from
  the cited trials' methods sections and public FDA/EMA assessment reports;
  they are illustrative surrogates, not a validated population-PK fit.
* The two-clone (epithelioid/sarcomatoid) tumor-growth structure is a
  simplification of the continuous histologic spectrum described in
  refs 8, 12-13; biphasic tumors are represented as a mixed fraction.
* For research, education, and hypothesis generation only — not for
  clinical dosing decisions.
