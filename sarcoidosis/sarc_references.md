## Model Notes

- Disease parameters calibrated to FIRST Trial (Infliximab, N=138, pulmonary sarcoidosis, 24-week primary endpoint FVC change: +2.5% vs placebo).
- Prednisone PK parameters: Bergrem et al., 1983; CL_predl ≈ 4.2 L/h, Vd_predl ≈ 105 L (70 kg adult).
- MTX polyglutamate dynamics based on: Bleyer 1978 and Schiff MH et al. Arthritis Rheum 1996;39(5):784-791.
- Granuloma burden scale (0-100 AU): 0-15 = Stage I (remission threshold), 15-40 = Stage II, 40-70 = Stage III, >70 = Stage IV.
- sIL-2R reference range: 223-710 U/mL (normal); >1000 U/mL in active sarcoidosis (sensitivity ~75%).
- Serum ACE reference range: 8-52 U/L; elevated in ~60% of active sarcoidosis.
