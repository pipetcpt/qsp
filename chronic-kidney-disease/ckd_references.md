## 1. Disease Pathophysiology & Progression

1. **Brenner BM et al.** (2001). *Remission of renal disease: Recounting the challenge, acquiring the goal.* Journal of Clinical Investigation.  
   [PMID 11404473](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/11404473/)

2. **Levey AS & Coresh J** (2012). *Chronic kidney disease.* Lancet, 379(9811):165–180.  
   [PMID 21840587](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/21840587/)

3. **Kidney Disease: Improving Global Outcomes (KDIGO)** (2024). *KDIGO 2024 CKD Guideline.* Kidney International Supplements.  
   [KDIGO Guidelines](https://kdigo.org/guidelines/ckd-evaluation-and-management/)  
   → Current staging system, eGFR + albuminuria heat map, treatment targets.

4. **Böhle A et al.** (1996). *Morphological correlations of glomerular and interstitial changes.* Kidney International.  
   [PMID 8788266](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/8788266/)  
   → Quantitative relationship between interstitial fibrosis and eGFR decline.

5. **Eddy AA** (2000). *Molecular basis of renal fibrosis.* Pediatric Nephrology, 15(3–4):290–301.  
   [PMID 11149131](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/11149131/)
