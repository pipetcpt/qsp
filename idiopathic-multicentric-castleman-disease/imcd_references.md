### Note on Model Calibration
The mrgsolve parameters in `imcd_mrgsolve_model.R` were anchored to:

- **Siltuximab PopPK** — Puchalski 2010 *Clin Cancer Res*, van Rhee 2014 *Lancet Oncol* (CONCERT)
- **Tocilizumab PopPK** — Frey 2010 *J Clin Pharmacol*, Saito 2017 *Br J Clin Pharmacol*
- **Sirolimus PopPK** — MacDonald 2000 *Clin Ther*
- **CRP/IL-6 dynamics** — Casper 2015 *Clin Cancer Res* biomarker substudy
- **TAFRO mTOR rationale** — Fajgenbaum 2019 *JCI*, Arenas 2020 *Blood*
- **Treatment guideline anchors** — van Rhee 2018 *Blood* (consensus), Pierson 2023 *Blood Adv* (real-world)

CDCN = Castleman Disease Collaborative Network · ACCELERATE = patient-powered natural history registry.
