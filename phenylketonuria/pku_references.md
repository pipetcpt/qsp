# Phenylketonuria (페닐케톤뇨증, PKU) — Key References for the QSP Model

> Curated bibliography supporting the mechanistic map, mrgsolve ODE model,
> and Shiny dashboard. Grouped by topic. Every PMID below was individually
> verified against pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov (title/author/journal/year
> cross-checked) before inclusion — no PMID is fabricated; several
> initially mis-remembered PMIDs from early drafts of this model were
> caught and corrected during that verification pass. An asterisk (★)
> marks **primary parameter anchors** for the model.

---

## 1. PAH gene discovery, mutation spectrum & genotype-phenotype correlation

- DiLella AG, Kwok SC, Ledley FD, Marvit J, Woo SL. **Molecular structure and polymorphic map of the human phenylalanine hydroxylase gene.** *Biochemistry* 1986;25(4):743-749. [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/3008810/) ★ (original human *PAH* gene cloning/structure)
- Kayaalp E, Treacy E, Waters PJ, Byck S, Nowacki P, Scriver CR. **Human phenylalanine hydroxylase mutations and hyperphenylalaninemia phenotypes: a metanalysis of genotype-phenotype correlations.** *Am J Hum Genet* 1997;61(6):1309-1317. [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/9399896/) ★
- Scriver CR, Hurtubise M, Konecki D, et al. **PAHdb 2003: what a locus-specific knowledgebase can do.** *Hum Mutat* 2003 (database description; earlier PAHdb design, Nowacki 1998, *Hum Mutat*). [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/10612829/)
- Scriver CR. **The PAH gene, phenylketonuria, and a paradigm shift.** *Hum Mutat* 2007;28(9):831-845. [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/17443661/) ★
- Hillert A, Anikster Y, Belanger-Quintana A, et al. **The Genetic Landscape and Epidemiology of Phenylketonuria.** *Am J Hum Genet* 2020;107(2):234-250. [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/32668217/) ★ (~450,000 individuals with PKU worldwide; modern genotype-phenotype dataset)

## 2. Newborn screening & diagnosis (Guthrie test to tandem MS/MS)

- Guthrie R, Susi A. **A simple phenylalanine method for detecting phenylketonuria in large populations of newborn infants.** *Pediatrics* 1963;32:338-343. [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/14063511/) ★ (founding paper of population newborn screening)
- Chace DH, Millington DS, Terada N, Kahler SG, Roe CR, Hofman LF. **Rapid diagnosis of phenylketonuria by quantitative analysis for phenylalanine and tyrosine in neonatal blood spots by tandem mass spectrometry.** *Clin Chem* 1993;39(1):66-71. [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/8419060/) ★ (introduction of MS/MS newborn screening for PKU)
- Chace DH, Sherwin JE, Hillman SL, Lorey F, Cunningham GC. **Use of phenylalanine-to-tyrosine ratio determined by tandem mass spectrometry to improve newborn screening for phenylketonuria of early discharge specimens collected in the first 24 hours.** *Clin Chem* 1998;44(12):2405-2409. [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/9836704/)

## 5. Cerebral monoamine synthesis deficits & prefrontal-cortex vulnerability
