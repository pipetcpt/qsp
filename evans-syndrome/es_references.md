# Evans Syndrome – QSP Model References

> Evans Syndrome (ES) = concurrent or sequential Autoimmune Hemolytic Anemia (AIHA) + Immune Thrombocytopenia (ITP)  
> This model covers pathophysiology, treatment PK/PD, and clinical endpoints.
