# Pyoderma Gangrenosum (PG) — Curated References

> Each item is grouped by mechanism / drug / clinical area. PubMed PMIDs are
> linked directly. PG remains an orphan disease with limited randomized trial
> data — case series and open-label cohorts dominate the evidence base.
