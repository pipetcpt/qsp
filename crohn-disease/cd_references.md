# Crohn's Disease QSP Model — References

Quantitative Systems Pharmacology model for Crohn's Disease (CD).
Mechanistic map: 134 nodes, 10 subgraph clusters.
mrgsolve model: 22-state ODE (10 drug PK + 12 disease PD compartments).
Shiny app: 7 interactive tabs.
